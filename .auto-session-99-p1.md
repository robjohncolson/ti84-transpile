# Auto-Session 99 P1: Disassemble 0x0921CB — ENTER handler post-DisarmScroll path

## Context
This is a TI-84 Plus CE ROM transpilation project. The ROM is at `TI-84_Plus_CE/ROM.rom` (4 MB eZ80). We have a transpiler at `scripts/transpile-ti84-rom.mjs` and an eZ80 decoder at `TI-84_Plus_CE/ez80-decoder.js`.

Session 98 confirmed the ENTER dispatch chain in the home-screen handler at 0x0585E9:
- After key comparison `cp 0x05` (kEnter), the handler calls 8 setup functions
- At step 54, it calls 0x099211 (DisarmScroll — a 29-byte scroll cleanup utility)
- After DisarmScroll returns, the handler executes:
  - `EI` at 0x05862A
  - `CALL 0x0921CB` at 0x05862B
- 0x0921CB starts with `CALL 0x08384B` (ChkFindSym area), then checks `LD A,(0xD01D0B); OR A; RET Z`
- The P1 probe from session 98 got stuck in 0x0827xx (InsertMem/allocator loops) for 200K steps after this call
