## Interpretation

- Legacy/default behavior pins both probe ports high at `0xff`, which keeps `0x006138` and `0x00613F` in their polling loops.
- The narrow fix is to return `0x00` from ports `0xd00d` and `0xd00c`. That clears the high nibble for the first loop and clears bit 2 for the second.
- A successful patched run should visit `0x006145` and avoid terminating on the poll blocks themselves.
